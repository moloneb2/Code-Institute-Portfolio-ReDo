About Us

Welcome to the Dublin Beekeepers Club!

Here we are passionate about the fascinating world of bees and beekeeping. Our club brings together a diverse group of individuals who share a common interest in bees, their conservation, and the art of beekeeping.

Feel free to browse through our website where you can see some great pictures and videos of our friends at work. Find out when and where we meet and sign up if you would like to join us

Junior Beekeepers

Are you a young enthusiast eager to explore the world of bees and beekeeping?

Junior Beekeepers offers you the opportunity to learn, grow, and make a positive impact on the environment. Join us to start your beekeeping adventure, make new friends, and become a part of a buzzing community dedicated to making a difference.

If you would like to be a junior beekeeper click here and complete the form. We will contact you as soon as possible

Our Location

We are located on the Clontarf Road, Dublin. The Clontarf road is situated on the north side of Dublin city. We have a large field where we have our bee avaries.
